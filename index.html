In 2021, I participated in a Scientific Initiation research project at IFSUL, focusing on school dropout rates in IFSUL schools. To achieve this, I worked with data analysis and Machine Learning, utilizing Python and the scikit-learn library to process and model the data. After thorough analysis, the research hypothesis—that distance was the primary factor influencing dropout—was tested. However, the results indicated that other factors had a greater impact.
